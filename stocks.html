Filters

RSI Badges

                

                    OB
                    Overbought — RSI ≥ 70
                    OS
                    Oversold — RSI ≤ 30
                    DIV↑
                    Bullish Divergence — Price higher low, RSI lower low (in oversold)
                    DIV↓
                    Bearish Divergence — Price higher high, RSI lower high (in overbought)
                    INC
                    Incomplete — one or more timeframes missing
                

            


            

                
Status Legend

                

                    🔥
                    Strong — All timeframes present and RSI > 60
                    ✅
                    Bullish — All timeframes present and RSI > 50
                    ⚠️
                    Mixed — Mixed signals, or incomplete data
                    ❌
                    Bearish — All timeframes present and RSI < 50
